Recently, a wave of loneliness always hit me just before the dusk came. It's a feeling where you just know that in the grand scheme of things, nothing ever matter, that you will die alone, that people are to absorb of themselves that they never thought of you. At that moment, I just want to cry and talk about it with somenoe. But, I know there's not really anyone I could talk about this with.

My friend, well... the polite way to say it is they lack utility for me. A friend is a friend but what is a friend if you can't hang on them at the time of need. I don't have many friends, far from a girlfriend or someone I could genuinely talk about this sort of things with. Everything I said to any of them will just in one ear and out the other.

To be honest, I don't know what to do about it. I don't see any escape to this at the moment, at least not in the near future.

It's not I lack purpose. I know what to do with my life. I have the roadmap. But the roadmap doesn't take into account this kind of moment. I just wish that serendipity of life will fix this for me.

To anyone read this, it would be a joy if you could reach out to me and see how things go. I could do with some talking. It's been a whlie since I do so.
